GOLDEN RETRIVER

The Golden Retriever is a large-sized breed of dog bred as gun dogs to retrieve shot waterfowl, such as ducks and upland game birds, during hunting and shooting parties, and were named 'retriever' because of their ability to retrieve shot game undamaged.

Life expectancy: 10 – 12 years
Colors: Dark Golden, Cream, Light Golden, Golden
Weight: Female: 25–32 kg, Male: 30–34 kg
Height: Female: 51–56 cm, Male: 56–61 cm
Temperament: Kind, Intelligent, Trustworthy, Reliable, Confident, Friendly






CORGI

The Pembroke Welsh Corgi is a cattle herding dog breed which originated in Pembrokeshire, Wales. It is one of two breeds known as a Welsh Corgi. The other is the Cardigan Welsh Corgi, and both descend from the line of northern spitz-type dogs.

Life expectancy: 12 – 15 years
Origin: Wales
Temperament: Tenacious,Bold,Outgoing,
Playful,Protective,Friendly
Weight: Male: 10–14 kg, Female: 10–13 kg
Colors: Fawn, Black & Tan,Sable,Black & White
Height: Male: 25–30 cm, Female: 25–30 cm






BEAGLE

The beagle is a breed of small hound that is similar in appearance to the much larger foxhound. The beagle is a scent hound, developed primarily for hunting hare.

Life expectancy: 12 – 15 years
Colors: Lemon & White, Chocolate Tri, White & Tan, Brown & White, Orange & White, Tri-color, Red & White
Temperament: Amiable, Intelligent, Determined, Excitable, Even Tempered, Gentle
Height: Male: 36–41 cm, Female: 33–38 cm
Weight: Male: 10–11 kg, Female: 9–10 kg
